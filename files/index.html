CAREER COUNSELLING WHAT WE DO IN CAREER COUNSELLING:

Career counselling service provides valuable insights and guidance to students to help them make informed decisions about their academic and career goals. This service helps students explore and identify their strengths and interests, and provides them with support and guidance throughout their educational journey, thus making them better equipped for success and fulfillment in their chosen careers.
